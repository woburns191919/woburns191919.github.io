About Me

I'm a software engineer with a PhD in Literature with a Creative Dissertation (a novel, currently under consideration). Recently, I've embarked on a new adventure in the tech world. Over the past year, I've dedicated myself to learning programming, a journey that has been both challenging and exhilarating. I've successfully completed two full-stack projects, showcasing my ability to not only learn a new skill set but also to apply it effectively. This foray into technology has equipped me with a unique perspective on storytelling, where code and narrative intersect. For 8 years, I had the privilege of teaching English at the university level. This experience allowed me to share my passion for literature with with students in Houston and also those in China who participated in the UH-Dalian Maritime University partnership. I developed courses that were as engaging as they were informative. My teaching philosophy centered around demystifying the writing process, making writing (be it creative, academic, or technical) accessible and relevant to all students. I am now at a thrilling crossroads, seeking opportunities where I can blend my background in literature and teaching with my newfound skills in programming. Whether it's developing engaging content, crafting unique digital experiences, or contributing to educational technology, I am excited to bring my diverse expertise to a dynamic team.
